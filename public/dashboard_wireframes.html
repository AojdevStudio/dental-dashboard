6. Technical Implementation Notes

Based on Current Data Structure:
  • hygiene_production: Daily tracking with hours, production estimates vs verified, goals, variance, and bonus calculations
  • dentist_production: Daily production tracking with efficiency metrics (per hour, daily averages, monthly goals)
  • providers: Link production data to specific dentists and hygienists
  • clinics: Multi-tenant support for different practice locations
  • Note: Office Manager specific metrics (like their bonuses) would need separate tables in future iterations

  • Warning Amber (#d97706) for alerts and targets
  • Professional white cards with subtle shadows
  • Inter font for optimal data readability
